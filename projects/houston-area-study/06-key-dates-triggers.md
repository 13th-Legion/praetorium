## Event-Based Triggers

### Police Killings

Any high-profile police killing of a Black individual, especially in Houston or Texas, will likely trigger:
- Immediate protests (within 24-48 hours)
- PSL, DSA, BLM coalition mobilization
- Potential for George Floyd comparison/escalation

### Immigration Enforcement

ICE raids, deportations, or immigration policy changes trigger:
- PSL organizing (anti-ICE focus)
- Vigils at detention centers
- Potential for escalation given Prairieland context

### Israel-Gaza Developments

Any escalation in Israel-Gaza conflict triggers:
- PSL mobilization (largest anti-Israel presence)
- Potential targeting of Jewish community events
- Documented antisemitic incidents at PSL events

### Supreme Court Decisions

Major SCOTUS decisions on abortion, LGBTQ+ rights, immigration trigger:
- Immediate protests
- Coalition mobilization
- Sustained campaign activity

### National Political Events

Presidential actions, congressional developments, major political events trigger:
- Rapid response protests
- PSL typically first to organize
