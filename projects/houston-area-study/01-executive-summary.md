## Key Findings

### Threat Environment

The Greater Houston metropolitan area presents a **moderate-to-elevated threat environment** for conservative political organizations. As the nation's fourth-largest metro and George Floyd's hometown, the region experienced significant civil unrest during 2020 and maintains active cells of multiple left-wing activist and armed groups. Houston also has notable right-wing extremist presence, including the site of Patriot Front's first public appearance.

**Primary Left-Wing Threat Actors:**
- **Antifa-affiliated networks** — Decentralized cells with documented presence; connected to broader Texas antifa networks implicated in Prairieland ICE attack
- **Party for Socialism and Liberation — Houston (PSL)** — Largest organized left presence with 15K Instagram followers; opening community organizing center in 2026; documented antisemitic incidents at protests
- **Houston DSA** — Democratic Socialists chapter with ~3K Instagram followers; focused on electoral/labor organizing
- **New Black Panther Nation** — Houston-based black nationalist group led by Quanell X; SPLC-classified hate group

**Secondary Left-Wing Actors:**
- BLM-affiliated organizations and activists (Trae tha Truth / Relief Gang)
- CPUSA Houston Club
- Various anarchist networks

**Right-Wing Actors (Contextual):**
- Patriot Front — Made first public appearance in Houston (Sept 2017)
- Proud Boys — Multiple Texas chapters active
- This Is Texas Freedom Force — FBI-designated militia active statewide
- Various anti-LGBTQ coalitions
