## Recommended Venue Characteristics

### Preferred

- Controlled access (single entry point manageable)
- Private parking (not street)
- Indoor venue (limits protest proximity)
- Professional security infrastructure
- Distance from synagogues/religious sites
- Not near major protest routes

### Avoid

- Venues adjacent to government buildings
- Downtown locations during high-risk dates
- Venues near religious facilities (especially synagogues)
- Open-air venues during elevated threat periods
- University campuses (First Amendment concerns)
