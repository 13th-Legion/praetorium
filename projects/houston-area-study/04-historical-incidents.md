## Incident Patterns

### Left-Wing Activity Patterns

1. **Coalition Protests:** PSL frequently organizes or co-organizes; DSA participates
2. **Mutual Aid Response:** Strong infrastructure developed post-Harvey; antifa networks involved
3. **Anti-Israel Actions:** Increasing since Oct 2023; documented antisemitic incidents
4. **Memorial Marches:** Organized around police killings; generally peaceful when coordinated with city

### Right-Wing Activity Patterns

1. **Propaganda Distribution:** Patriot Front active in Houston area
2. **Anti-Drag Protests:** Coalition activity at LGBTQ+ events
3. **Monument Defense:** Historical activity around Confederate monuments

### Terrorism Patterns

1. **Islamist Radicalization:** Multiple Houston-area residents involved in recent plots/attacks
2. **Online Radicalization:** Anas Said case shows post-Oct 7 pathway
3. **Diverse Targeting:** Religious sites (synagogues, churches, temples) all targeted

## Lessons Learned

### From 2020 Protests

- **Coordination with city leadership reduces violence:** Memorial march with Mayor Turner was peaceful vs. initial protests
- **Celebrity/influencer involvement matters:** Trae tha Truth and Bun B provided credible organizing leadership
- **HPD use of force creates legal liability:** Multiple lawsuits followed mounted officer incident
- **Houston can mobilize massive crowds:** 60,000+ at memorial march

### From Terrorism Incidents

- **Local radicalization is occurring:** Multiple Houston residents involved in Islamist-inspired violence
- **Houses of worship are targets:** Churches, synagogues, temples all affected
- **Vehicle attacks are a methodology:** Bourbon Street attacker from Houston used rental truck

### From Right-Wing Activity

- **Houston was early organizing ground:** Patriot Front's first appearance here
- **Propaganda operations ongoing:** Flyer distribution continues
- **Less direct confrontation than DFW:** Fewer armed standoffs at events
