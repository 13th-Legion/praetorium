# Risk Assessment

## 8.1 Threat Scenario Analysis

### Scenario 1: Protest Disruption at Public Event

**Description:** Activists mobilize for peaceful demonstration outside event, focusing on environmental or immigration issues.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | MEDIUM |
| **Impact** | LOW |
| **Overall Risk** | **MODERATE** |

**Mitigations:**
- Venue with buffer
- LE coordination

---

### Scenario 2: Physical Confrontation

**Description:** Verbal escalation to minor physical altercation.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | LOW |
| **Impact** | MEDIUM |
| **Overall Risk** | **LOW** |

**Mitigations:**
- De-escalation training
- Avoid engagement
