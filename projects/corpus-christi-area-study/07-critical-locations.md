## 7.4 Venues for Political Events

**Higher Risk Venues:**
- Public parks like Bayfront (accessible)
- Downtown hotels near Marina

**Lower Risk Venues:**
- Suburban facilities in Portland
- Private venues with controlled access

**Security Features:**
- Controlled entry
- Private parking
- Buffer from public areas

---

## 7.5 Areas of Concern

**Higher Density:**
- Downtown/Bayfront
- Near port/refineries

**Lower Density:**
- Suburban areas
- North Padre Island

## 7.6 Route Planning

**Considerations:**
- Avoid Shoreline Blvd during events
- Use SH-358 for access
- Plan alternatives near NAS/port
