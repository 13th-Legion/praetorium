## Planning Calendar

### High-Risk Windows (Avoid if Possible)

1. **May 1 ± 3 days** — May Day
2. **June weekends** — Pride events
3. **July 4 ± 3 days** — Independence Day / Prairieland anniversary
4. **November 3 ± 7 days** — Election period

### Lower-Risk Windows

1. **Late December - early January** — Holiday period; reduced activity
2. **August** — Hot weather; reduced outdoor activity
3. **Mid-week dates** — Lower mobilization than weekends
4. **Early morning events** — Activists mobilize later in day
