# Recommendations

## 9.1 Physical Security Measures

### Event Security

**Minimum Standards:**
- Professional security
- LE coordination
- Controlled entry

**Enhanced:**
- Screening, medical support

**High-Risk:**
- Executive protection, advance surveys

### Venue Selection

**Prioritize:**
- Suburban, controlled access

**Avoid:**
- Capitol vicinity, UT area

---

## 9.2 Event Planning Considerations

**Pre-Event:**
- Venue selection, monitoring

**During:**
- Perimeter watch

**Post:**
- Debrief, monitor doxxing

---

## 9.3 OPSEC for Members

**Personal:**
- Social media hygiene
- At events: masks, carpool

**Leadership:**
- Home security, routines vary

---

## 9.4 Monitoring & Early Warning

**Establish:**
- Alerts, social tracking

**Indicators:**
- Mobilization calls

---

## 9.5 Law Enforcement Coordination

- Build relationships with APD, DPS
- Request patrols, intel

---

## 9.6 Legal Preparedness

- Retain counsel for protest law
- Insurance review

---

## 9.7 Summary: Priority Actions

**Immediate:**
- Monitoring setup
- Security vendor

**Near-Term:**
- SOP development
- Training

**Ongoing:**
- Assessments, updates
