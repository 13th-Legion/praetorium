## 6.2 Reactive Triggers

### High-Impact Triggers

**Police Incidents:**
- Shootings or force against minorities

**Legislative Actions:**
- Bills on abortion, trans rights, immigration

**Federal Policies:**
- ICE raids, deportations

**National Events:**
- SCOTUS rulings, political violence

### Moderate-Impact Triggers

**Local Government:**
- APD budget votes, oversight hearings

**Campus Events:**
- UT policies on speech, divestment
