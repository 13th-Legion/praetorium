# Threat Landscape

## 3.1 Left-Wing Activist Groups (Primary Focus)

### DSA San Antonio

**Overview:**  
The San Antonio chapter of Democratic Socialists of America is an active local organization focused on socialist organizing in South Texas.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Emphasis on labor rights, housing justice, and electoral campaigns
- ~2,500 Facebook followers; regular meetings and events
- Collaborates with other progressive groups
- Not primarily confrontational but mobilizes for protests

**San Antonio Activities:**
- Support for tenant unions and anti-eviction campaigns
- Endorsements of progressive candidates
- Participation in immigration and police reform rallies
- Mutual aid programs

**Threat Assessment:**  
Primarily political; may provide logistical support for demonstrations.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Capability | LOW-MEDIUM |
| Intent | LOW |
| Current Activity | MODERATE |
| Overall Threat | **LOW** |

---

### Black Lives Matter Affiliates

**Overview:**  
BLM in San Antonio operates through informal networks rather than a formal chapter, focusing on police accountability and racial justice.

**Key Groups:**
- Local organizers from 2020 protests
- Coalitions with groups like ACT 4 SA
- Partnerships with national BLM for campaigns

**Current Status:**  
Activity peaked in 2020; now focuses on SAPD oversight, community events, and anniversaries.

**Tactics:**
- Peaceful marches and vigils
- Social media advocacy
- Coalition-building with other movements
- Occasional disruptions at city council meetings

**Threat Assessment:**

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Capability | MEDIUM |
| Intent | LOW-MEDIUM |
| Current Activity | LOW-MODERATE |
| Overall Threat | **LOW-MODERATE** |

---

### John Brown Gun Club Elements

**Overview:**  
Armed left-wing group with documented presence in San Antonio, part of national network.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Provides "community defense" at events
- Openly armed where legal
- Overlaps with other leftist groups

**San Antonio Activities:**
- Armed presence at 2022 drag show protests
- Counter-protests against right-wing demonstrators
- Training and education focus

**Threat Assessment:**

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Capability | MEDIUM-HIGH |
| Intent | MEDIUM |
| Current Activity | LOW |
| Overall Threat | **MODERATE** |
