# Recommendations

## 9.1 Physical Security Measures

### Event Security

**Minimum Standards (All Events):**
- Professional security
- LE coordination
- Controlled entry
- Comms plan
- Emergency plan

**Enhanced (Elevated Risk):**
- Uniformed LE
- Screening
- Medical support

**High-Risk:**
- Executive protection
- Advance survey
- Secure transport

### Venue Selection

**Prioritize:**
- Private, controlled
- Enclosed parking
- Buffer zones
- Away from downtown/Alamo

**Avoid:**
- Historic downtown
- Near JBSA gates
- Public parks

---

## 9.2 Event Planning Considerations

**Pre-Event Timelines:**
- 4+ weeks: Venue, LE
- 2 weeks: Security plan
- 1 week: Monitoring
- 48 hrs: Threat check

**During/Post:**
- Awareness
- Debrief
- Monitor doxxing

---

## 9.3 OPSEC for Members

**Personal:**
- Social media privacy
- At events: Masks, no identifiers
- General: Self-audits

**Leadership:**
- Home security
- Vary routines

---

## 9.4 Monitoring & Early Warning

**Establish:**

- Mobilization calls
