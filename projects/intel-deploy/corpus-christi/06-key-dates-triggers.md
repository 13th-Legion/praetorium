## 6.2 Reactive Triggers

### High-Impact Triggers

**Border Incidents:**
- ICE raids or deportations
- Migrant crises in South Texas

**Environmental Incidents:**
- Refinery accidents, spills
- New industrial project approvals

**Police Incidents:**
- CCPD use of force cases
- National police brutality events

### Moderate-Impact Triggers

**Local Government Actions:**
- City Council votes on development
- Port authority decisions

**National Events:**
- Immigration policy changes
- Environmental regulations

---

## 6.3 Client-Specific Triggers

Events may trigger response if:
- Supporting energy industry
- Near NAS or port
- On immigration topics
