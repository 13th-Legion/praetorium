## 7.3 Venues for Political Events

### Considerations for Site Selection

**Higher Risk Venues:**
- Downtown hotels/convention centers (accessible, visible)
- Public parks (no access control)
- University campuses (student activist base)
- Venues with prior protest history

**Lower Risk Venues:**
- Suburban hotels with controlled access
- Private clubs/facilities
- Exurban locations (reduced activist presence)
- Venues with dedicated parking (vs. street parking)

### Venue Security Features to Prioritize

1. **Controlled access** — Single entry point, security screening capability
2. **Private parking** — Enclosed garage or fenced lot; prevents vehicle documentation
3. **Buffer zone** — Distance from public sidewalk to venue entrance
4. **Secondary egress** — Alternative exit routes for VIPs/speakers
5. **Law enforcement relationship** — Venue with existing LE coordination
6. **No prior incident history** — Avoid venues that have hosted controversial events

## 7.4 Areas of Concern

### Incident Density Mapping

Based on historical data, conservative event planners should be aware of elevated risk in:

**Highest Incident Density:**
- Downtown Dallas (Main/Elm/Commerce corridors)
- Downtown Fort Worth (Sundance Square area)
- Deep Ellum entertainment district
- Areas near ICE facilities

**Moderate Incident Density:**
- Uptown Dallas
- Oak Lawn (LGBTQ+ neighborhood)
- University areas (SMU, UNT, TCU)
- Inner-ring suburbs with diverse politics

**Lower Incident Density:**
- Outer suburbs (Frisco, McKinney, Southlake, Keller)
- Exurban areas
- Strongly Republican counties (Rockwall, Ellis)

## 7.5 Route Planning

### Considerations for Event Transportation

**Arrival/Departure:**
- Avoid routes through downtown cores when possible
- Identify protest-free ingress/egress routes
- Have alternative routes planned
- Consider timing to avoid peak protest hours (typically afternoon-evening)

**VIP/Speaker Transportation:**
- Dedicated vehicles with security
- Non-public arrival points (loading docks, private entrances)
- Pre-positioned vehicles for rapid departure
- Counter-surveillance awareness

**Attendee Guidance:**
- Clear parking instructions (avoid street parking in protest-prone areas)
- Recommended routes avoiding potential choke points
- Communication plan for route changes day-of
