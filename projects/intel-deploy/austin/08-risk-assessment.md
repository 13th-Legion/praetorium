# Risk Assessment

## 8.1 Threat Scenario Analysis

### Scenario 1: Protest Disruption at Public Event

**Description:** Activists mobilize to protest outside event, chanting and blocking access.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | HIGH |
| **Impact** | MEDIUM |
| **Overall Risk** | **ELEVATED** |

**Mitigations:** Venue selection, LE coordination.
