## 7.3 Venues for Political Events

**Higher Risk:**
- Downtown hotels, public parks
- Near Capitol or UT

**Lower Risk:**
- Suburban venues with access control
- Private facilities in Williamson County

**Security Features:**
- Controlled entry, private parking
- Buffer from public areas

---

## 7.4 Areas of Concern

**Highest Density:**
- Central Austin, Capitol grounds
- UT campus

**Moderate:**
- East Austin, South Congress

**Lower:**
- Northern suburbs, exurban areas

---

## 7.5 Route Planning

**Arrival/Departure:**
- Avoid I-35 through downtown
- Use alternative routes

**VIP Transport:**
- Secure vehicles, private entrances

**Attendee Guidance:**
- Designated parking, route info
