# Threat Landscape

## 3.1 Left-Wing Activist Groups (Primary Focus)

### Palestinian Solidarity Committee (PSC) - UT Austin

**Overview:**  
Student-led group at the University of Texas focused on pro-Palestinian advocacy and anti-Israel campaigns. Active in campus protests demanding university divestment.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Organizes walkouts, sit-ins, and encampments
- Collaborates with national networks like Students for Justice in Palestine
- Emphasizes free speech and anti-imperialism
- Members often overlap with other progressive student groups

**Austin Presence:**  
Led major protests in 2024, including April encampment resulting in over 50 arrests. Continues organizing rallies and solidarity events.

**Threat Assessment:**  
High mobilization capacity on campus; potential for disruption at events perceived as pro-Israel or conservative.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Capability | MEDIUM (large student turnout) |
| Intent | HIGH (direct action focus) |
| Current Activity | ACTIVE |
| Overall Threat | **ELEVATED** |

### Red Guard Austin (Historical)

**Overview:**  
Maoist collective active from 2015-2022, known for militant tactics and disruptions of other leftist groups.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Engaged in sectarian conflicts and aggressive direct actions
- Disbanded amid allegations of cult-like leadership
- Historical significance in Austin's far-left scene

**Current Status:**  
No longer active, but former members may participate in other groups. Legacy influences current militant activism.

**Threat Assessment:**

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Capability | LOW (disbanded) |
| Intent | HIGH (historical) |
| Current Activity | INACTIVE |
| Overall Threat | **LOW** |

### Single-Issue Activist Groups

**Defund APD / Police Oversight Movements:**
