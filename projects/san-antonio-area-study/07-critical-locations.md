## 7.3 Venues for Political Events

### Considerations for Site Selection

**Higher Risk Venues:**
- Downtown historic sites (accessible)
- Public parks (no control)
- Near military bases (federal issues)

**Lower Risk Venues:**
- Suburban hotels
- Private facilities
- Exurban locations

### Venue Security Features

1. Controlled access
2. Private parking
3. Buffer zones
4. Secondary exits
5. LE coordination
6. No protest history

---

## 7.4 Areas of Concern

**Highest Density:**
- Downtown (Alamo, City Hall)
- Near SAPD HQ

**Moderate:**
- Near JBSA installations
- University areas

**Lower:**
- Outer suburbs (Boerne, Schertz)

## 7.5 Route Planning

**Arrival/Departure:**
- Avoid downtown routes
- Alternative paths
- Timing to evade peaks

**VIP Transport:**
- Secure vehicles
- Private entrances
- Rapid exit plans

**Attendee Guidance:**
- Parking instructions
- Route recommendations
- Change communication
