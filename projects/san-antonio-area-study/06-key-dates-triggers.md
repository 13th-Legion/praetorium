## 6.2 Reactive Triggers

### High-Impact Triggers

**Immigration Enforcement Actions:**
- ICE raids, deportations
- Border policy changes
- Military deployments to border

**Police Incidents:**
- SAPD use of force cases
- Shootings involving minorities

**Court Decisions:**
- Rulings on immigration, abortion, LGBTQ+
- Local police reform cases

**Political Violence:**
- Attacks on activists
- Mass shootings with political motives

### Moderate-Impact Triggers

**Legislative Actions:**
- Texas lege on border security, policing

**Local Government:**
- City council on SAPD contracts
- JBSA-related decisions

**National Events:**
- Presidential visits to Texas
- Major protests with local solidarity
