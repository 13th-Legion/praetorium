# Risk Assessment

## 8.1 Threat Scenario Analysis

### Scenario 1: Protest Disruption at Public Event

**Description:** Activists mobilize to protest outside event, chanting, blocking, harassing attendees.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | HIGH |
| **Impact** | MEDIUM |
| **Overall Risk** | **ELEVATED** |

**Indicators:** Social media calls, counter-events.

**Mitigations:** Limited publicity, venue buffer, LE coordination.

---

### Scenario 2: Physical Confrontation

**Description:** Escalation to physical altercations.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | MEDIUM |
| **Impact** | HIGH |
| **Overall Risk** | **ELEVATED** |

**Indicators:** Armed groups like JBGC.

**Mitigations:** Security de-escalation, attendee instructions.

---

### Scenario 3: Doxxing Campaign

**Description:** Identification and online exposure of attendees.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | HIGH |
| **Impact** | MEDIUM |
| **Overall Risk** | **ELEVATED** |

**Indicators:** Photographers at events.

**Mitigations:** OPSEC, private parking.

---

### Scenario 4: Armed Standoff

**Description:** Armed activists confront security.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | LOW |
| **Impact** | HIGH |
| **Overall Risk** | **MODERATE** |

**Indicators:** JBGC mobilization.

**Mitigations:** LE presence, avoid flashpoint topics.

---

### Scenario 5: Targeted Violence

**Description:** Assault or worse against participants.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | LOW |
| **Impact** | CRITICAL |
| **Overall Risk** | **MODERATE** |

**Indicators:** Specific threats.

**Mitigations:** Screening, threat monitoring.

---

### Scenario 6: Infiltration

**Description:** Activist joins to disrupt or gather intel.

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Likelihood** | LOW-MED |
| **Impact** | MEDIUM |
| **Overall Risk** | **MODERATE** |

**Indicators:** Suspicious new members.

**Mitigations:** Vetting, compartmentalization.
