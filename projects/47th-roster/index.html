About the 47th

The 47th Legion was founded in 2007 by a group of gamers seeking more than just a casual clan. We wanted structure, camaraderie, and a place where dedication was recognized and rewarded.

Over the years, we've fought in countless games, weathered roster changes, and built something that's lasted longer than most gaming communities ever do. Our Roman-inspired rank structure isn't just for show — it reflects our values of discipline, honor, and earned respect.

Whether you're a grizzled veteran or fresh recruit, if you value teamwork and want to be part of something bigger, the 47th might be your home.
