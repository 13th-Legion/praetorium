The honors and structure of the 47th Legion

⚔️ RANK STRUCTURE

The 47th Legion uses a Roman-inspired military rank structure. Junior enlisted ranks (E-1 to E-3) are earned through time in service, while senior enlisted (E-4+) and Officer ranks require demonstrated leadership and are awarded by command.

📈 Automatic Promotion Schedule

  • E-1 Tiron → E-2 Miles: 30 days of service
  • E-2 Miles → E-3 Miles Gregarius: 60 days of service
  • E-3 Miles Gregarius → E-4 Decanus: 120 days of service
  • E-5+ Senior Enlisted: Awarded by command based on merit
  • Officer Ranks (O-1 to O-8): Appointed by Legion command

🎖️ DECORATIONS & AWARDS

Legionaries earn awards for valor, service, and participation in campaigns. Awards are displayed in order of precedence on member profiles.

Valor Decorations

Corona Obsidionalis
Breaking the siege of beleaguered Imperial forces.
Corona Civica
Saving the life of another Legionary.
Legionary's Medal
Distinguishing heroism above and beyond the call of duty.
Corona Aurea
Killing an enemy soldier in single combat.
Corona Vallaris
First Legionary to enter an enemy fortification.
Corona Muralis
First Legionary to enter a besieged city.
Wounded in Combat
Suffer egregious physical injury from enemy contact.
Torques
Display valor in combat.
