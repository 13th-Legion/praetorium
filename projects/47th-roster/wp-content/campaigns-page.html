⚔️ Active Deployments

The Legion is currently operating in the following theaters of war:

Dune: Awakening

Survive the deserts of Arrakis in this open-world survival MMO. Build shelters, spice harvesters, and join the fight for control of the most valuable substance in the universe. The spice must flow.

Fallout 76

Explore the post-apocalyptic wasteland of Appalachia with your fellow Legionnaires. Build camps, complete events, and survive the nuclear frontier together. War never changes.

Aliens: Fireteam Elite

Set in the iconic Alien universe, join your fireteam against waves of terrifying Xenomorph and Weyland-Yutani synthetic threats. Cooperative survival shooter action at its finest.

War Thunder

Command tanks, aircraft, and naval vessels across historic battlefields from WWII to the modern era. Free-to-play vehicle combat with unmatched depth and realism.
